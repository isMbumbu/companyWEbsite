About Us

Itothya General Agencies Ltd. is a reputable debt collection agency in Kenya. With our tax compliance certification and insurance coverage through Africa Merchant Assurance Company, we offer secure and professional debt recovery services. We prioritize integrity, confidentiality, and excellence in every aspect of our work.

Our Services

  • Debt Recovery/Collection: Customized debt collection for both retail and corporate clients.
  • Investigation / Skip Tracing: Tracing debtors with our extensive field experience.
  • Process Server: Efficient and professional document service.
  • Asset Searches: Skilled investigators accessing public information databases.
  • Debt Surveillance: Monitoring financial changes in written-off debts.
  • Consultancy: Advice on credit policies and debt collection training.
  • Reconciliation: Efficient reconciliation services to save client resources.
